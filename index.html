Why it Matters

Every year, thousands of lives are lost to preventable accidents caused by distracted driving. This website aims to make a difference by encouraging people to put their phones away and focus on the road.

Did You Know?

  • Texting while driving causes 1.6 million crashes annually.
  • It only takes 5 seconds of distraction to cover the length of a football field at 55 mph.
  • Teen drivers are especially vulnerable, making education crucial.

Get Involved

Join our mission by pledging to never text and drive. Encourage your friends and family to do the same. Together, we can make our roads safer.

Tips for Staying Safe

  1. Enable "Do Not Disturb While Driving" mode on your phone.
  2. Put your phone out of reach while driving.
  3. Ask passengers to handle calls and messages for you.
