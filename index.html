Welcome to Focus26

Texting while driving is one of the most common and deadly distractions on the road today. Our mission is to spread awareness and provide resources to prevent accidents and save lives.

Did you know that drivers who text are 23 times more likely to crash? A single message can change everything. Let’s commit to safer roads together.



Tips for Staying Safe


  1. Enable "Do Not Disturb While Driving" mode on your phone.
  2. Put your phone out of reach while driving.
  3. Ask passengers to handle calls and messages for you.
