About the Site

Although it's crucial to avoid texting while driving, this site was created as part of an assignment for IMD1005 Web Development. My name is Vinit Rao, and at the time of creating this site, I am in my first-year winter term. The motivation behind this project comes from my experience of reckless driving among teens around my age, an issue I've noticed throughout high school and into university. Texting while driving is just one part of this problem, but it's an especially preventable and dangerous behavior.
